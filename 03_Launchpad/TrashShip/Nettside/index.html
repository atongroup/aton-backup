The problem
"The ocean is not a dumping ground.
We intend to prove it."

Every minute of every day, the equivalent of a garbage truck of plastic enters the ocean. It doesn't disappear — it breaks down, spreads, and works its way into the food chain. Every solution so far has been too small, too slow, or too dependent on returning to shore. TrashShip is being designed from the ground up to solve a problem at the scale it actually exists.
