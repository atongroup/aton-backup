Unsere Philosophie

A memorial should tell
a story — not just
mark a place.

The memorial industry has been dominated by granite for over a hundred years. Not because it is the best choice, but because no one has challenged it. 68% of all granite is imported from China and Vietnam.

In Norway, more than half of all who die are now cremated (51.4% in 2024). Families deserve something worth keeping — beautiful enough to stand in the living room, not hidden away in a cupboard.

Velumen gründet auf einer Überzeugung: Die Menschen, die wir lieben, lebten in Farben, im Licht, in Materialien mit Charakter. Das Grabmal und die Urne sollten das widerspiegeln.

We use recycled glass, corten steel and locally sourced Norwegischer Stein — materials with 70%+ lower CO₂ footprint than imported granite. The glass is mouth-blown by hand on Jæren. The steel is cut and welded in Stavanger. The stone comes from Norwegian quarries. And we integrate NFC memory technology that lets families preserve a whole life, not just two dates.
