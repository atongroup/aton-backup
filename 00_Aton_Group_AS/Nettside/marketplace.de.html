Quality standard
What Aton-Certified
means for buyers

Every listing on Aton Launchpad has passed our internal quality check before going live. This is what you can expect when you see the ✦ badge.

  • Market analysis with TAM/SAM/SOM and cited sources
  • Pitch deck reviewed and investor-ready
  • Website live and tested
  • At least one funding application prepared
  • Legal templates (NDA + VB agreement) included
  • Supplier or channel contacts identified
  • Concept note with honest valuation range
  • Aton board seat included — you are never alone
What it is not
Aton-Certified does not mean revenue, customers, or a signed LOI. These are pre-launch concepts — the structure is done, the market is real, the execution is yours.
