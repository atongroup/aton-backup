Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Non-Disclosure Agreement (the «Agreement») is entered into between Aton Group AS, organisation number under registration, domiciled in Stavanger, Norway («Aton»), and the applicant submitting through this portal («Founder»).

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to protect confidential information shared by the Founder with Aton during the evaluation of a potential business partnership. The Founder retains full ownership of all intellectual property at all times.

2. Definition of Confidential Information

«Confidential Information» means all information relating to the Founder's business idea, including but not limited to: business concepts, technical descriptions, market analyses, financial projections, prototypes, trade secrets and all documents uploaded through this portal.

3. Aton's Obligations

Aton undertakes to: (a) keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential; (b) not disclose any Confidential Information to third parties without the Founder's prior written consent; (c) use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential partnership with the Founder; (d) take all reasonable measures to protect the Confidential Information against unauthorised access or disclosure.

4. Intellectual Property

Nothing in this Agreement transfers any intellectual property rights from the Founder to Aton. The Founder retains all rights, title and interest in and to the Confidential Information and any intellectual property contained therein. Aton acquires no licence or right to use the Founder's intellectual property except for the limited purpose of evaluation as set out in this Agreement.

5. Term

The confidentiality obligations in this Agreement shall remain in force for a period of five (5) years from the date of submission of the application.

6. Breach and Remedies

In the event of a breach of this Agreement by Aton, Aton shall pay the Founder liquidated damages in the amount of NOK 500 000 (five hundred thousand Norwegian kroner). The Founder shall also be entitled to seek injunctive relief and any other remedies available under Norwegian law.

7. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Norway. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Stavanger District Court (Stavanger tingrett).

8. Electronic Signature

The Founder's typed name in the application form constitutes a valid and binding electronic signature in accordance with the Norwegian Electronic Signatures Act (esignaturloven). Submission of the form constitutes the Founder's acceptance of this Agreement.
